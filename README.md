# 🚦 Distribution of Traffic Violations by Congestion Level

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Traffic Risk Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze how traffic violations vary across different congestion levels, weather conditions, and time patterns.

The data was processed using **SQL and Excel**, and visualized in Power BI to extract meaningful insights that can support better traffic management and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze the distribution of traffic violations across congestion levels  
- Understand the impact of weather conditions on traffic risk  
- Identify peak hours with higher traffic risk  
- Explore relationships between speed, temperature, and congestion  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data extraction, querying, and transformation  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and structuring  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard design, visualization, and interactivity  
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and measures  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Traffic violations analysis by congestion level  
- Weather-based risk and congestion insights  
- Hourly traffic speed trends  
- KPI cards (Average Speed, Risk Count, etc.)  
- Interactive filters for dynamic analysis  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Higher congestion levels are associated with increased traffic violations  
- Speed and signal violations are more frequent in high congestion areas  
- Rainy and cloudy weather conditions contribute to higher traffic risk  
- Average speed decreases as congestion increases  
- Peak hours show significantly higher traffic risk
